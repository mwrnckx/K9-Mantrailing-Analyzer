// capabilities

Everything your team needs

Built specifically for mantrailing and practical scent tracking — not adapted from generic GPS tools.

🎯

Accuracy Analysis

Measures your dog's deviation from the runner's trail using weighted GPS metrics that account for scent cone dynamics — not just raw distance.

⚡

Speed & Efficiency

Calculates weighted working speed along the completed trail. Fast teams that stay accurate score well. Fast teams that drift don't.

🐕

Handler Trail-Reading Score

Unique checkpoint system evaluates whether you can correctly identify when your dog is on scent — the skill that separates good handlers from great ones.

🎬

GPS Overlay Video

Generates a professional overlay showing dog vs. runner track, wind direction, deviation, and key stats — ready to sync with your action camera footage.

🏆

Competition Scoring

Full double-blind scoring system designed for practical tracking competitions. Grounded in mantrailing methodology, not adapted from sport tracking formats.

📊

Training Statistics

Track your team's progress over time. Analyse trail length, age, speed distribution, and deviation trends across multiple training sessions.

// workflow

Up and running in minutes

No account, no subscription, no cloud. Everything runs locally on your Windows PC.

Download & unzip

Download the ZIP from Releases, unzip to any folder, run K9TrailsAnalyzer.exe. No installer needed.

Try the sample files

Sample GPX files are included. Open them immediately to see a full analysis without needing your own training data.

Load your GPX tracks

Import the runner's trail and the dog's GPS track. Both recorded on standard phones — no special hardware required.

Analyse & export

Review accuracy, speed and checkpoint scores. Export an overlay video to review the trail with your training group.
